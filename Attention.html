What is the Stroop Effect?
The Stroop Effect is a psychological phenomonen that correlates reaction time to interference in a famililar environment.
It was first discovered by John Ridley Stroop in 1935.
Before starting the game, it is recommended you read more about the Stroop Effect .
Colour Test Instructions: + -
For each word, select the COLOUR of the word from the options. There are 15 words and you have 2 seconds for each word! Your score will be displayed at the end. Good Luck!
Numerical Test Instructions: + -
There will be two numbers displayed on the screen. Select the number that is bigger in VALUE (not necessarily size). There are 15 questions and you have 2 seconds for each question! Your score will be displayed at the end. Good Luck!
